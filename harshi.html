SRI VENKATESWARA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering(svce),tirupati is a premier institution known for its excellence in technical education and innovation. Established with a vision to nurture future engineering.Sri venkateswara college of engineering(svce),tirupati is a leading institution under the Council for Social Development Trust, affiliated with JNTU Anantapur and accredited by NAAC (A Grade)and NBA. It offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA programs with state-of-the-art infrastructure, research centers, and strong industry collaborations. With a focus on quality education, innovation, and placements, SVCE prepares students for successful careers in top companies like Infosys, TCS, and Wipro. Under the leadership of Dr.N.Sudhakar Reddy, the college continues to excel in technical education and research.


SVCE is recognized for its academic excellence and is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Anantapur.It holds accreditations from NAAC and AICTE, ensuring high standards of education. Over the years, the college has earned a reputation for producing industry-ready professionals, making it one of the top engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh.
